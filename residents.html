Getting Started

New Resident Checklist

Follow these steps to get fully set up in Legacy Park. Most can be done in your first week.

1

Complete the Access Card Waiver

Before visiting the office, each resident wishing to receive an amenity access card must complete the online waiver. Complete the waiver form →

2

Visit the HOA Office for Your Welcome Packet

Stop by 4201 Legacy Park Circle (lower level of the Clubhouse). Staff will create your Legacy Park ID and provide your welcome packet. Hours: Mon–Fri 10am–2pm, 1st Saturday 9–11am.

3

Register in the Owner Portal

The Owner Portal is our primary means of staying connected. Register with your primary email address — accounts are limited to one email. Don't have access? Email HOAoffice@LegacyPark.org.

4

Explore Your Amenities

4 swimming pools, 11 tennis courts, pickleball courts, outdoor fitness, nature trails, and much more. All are available to you as a Legacy Park resident.

5

Check the Events Calendar & Sign Up for Sports

Our full-time Activities Director hosts events year-round — most are free. Youth Soccer, T-Ball, Tennis, Pickleball, and more are registerable at LeagueApps.

6

Subscribe to the Newsletter

Stay informed via our Constant Contact email newsletter. Subscribe here →
