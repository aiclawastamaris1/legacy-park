Getting Started

New Resident Checklist

Follow these steps to get fully set up in Legacy Park. Most can be done in your first week.

1

Visit the HOA Office for Your Welcome Packet

Stop by 4201 Legacy Park Circle (lower level of the Clubhouse). The HOA staff will give you your welcome packet and create your Legacy Park IDs. Each resident wishing to obtain an access card must complete the waiver before visiting. Office hours: Mon–Fri 10am–2pm, 1st Saturday 9–11am.

→ Complete Access Card Waiver Form first

2

Register in the Owner Portal

The Resident Center (Owner Portal) is our primary means of staying connected with homeowners. Register online — you'll need your primary email address. Owner accounts are limited to ONE email address.

Don't have an account? Email HOAoffice@LegacyPark.org to get set up.

3

Explore Your Amenities

You now have access to 4 swimming pools, 11 tennis courts, miles of trails, pickleball courts, a cultural amphitheater, and much more. Browse the full list and learn the rules for each amenity.

4

Check the Events Calendar

Our full-time Activities Director hosts events throughout the year — most are free for residents. Check the calendar to see what's coming up.

→ View Event Calendar

5

Register for Sports Programs

Youth Soccer, T-Ball, Adult Soccer, Tennis, Pickleball, Volleyball, and Travel Soccer are all available. Most use LeagueApps for registration.

→ See All Sports Programs

6

Subscribe to the Newsletter

Stay informed about community news, events, and important updates via Constant Contact email newsletter.

→ Subscribe to Newsletter
