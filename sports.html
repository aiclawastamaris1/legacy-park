🏆 Competitive

Recreational Travel Soccer (LPSC)

What began as a small neighborhood team in 2019 rapidly evolved into a formidable program. By Fall 2020 LPSC expanded to two championship-caliber teams. The program boasts multiple Divisional Championships, over a dozen Tournament Championships, and multiple Tournament final appearances.

"Above all, we are a family — one that plays together, grows together, and supports one another unconditionally."
