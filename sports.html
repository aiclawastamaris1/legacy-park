⚽ Sports & Recreation

From youth leagues to adult pick-up games, Legacy Park has one of the most active sports communities in Kennesaw. A resident amenity ID is required for all facilities.

Amenity ID Required: A Legacy Park Amenity ID card must be carried at all times when using community sports facilities. Residents must be present with any non-resident guests and stay within guest ratio limits.

Tennis
11 courts · Director Clifford Malivert · Lessons available

Legacy Park's 11 tennis courts are for the exclusive use of residents and their guests. A resident must be on the court with any non-resident guests and be within the guest ratio. Members must carry their amenity ID card at all times.

Meet your Tennis Director, Clifford Malivert — 30+ years of experience. Cliff has worked with ATP and WTA professionals and brings elite-level coaching to the Legacy Park community.

Recreational Travel Soccer
LPSC · Divisional & Tournament Champions

What began as a small neighborhood team in 2019 has rapidly evolved into a formidable program making a significant impact on competition across the state of Georgia.

By Fall 2020, LPSC expanded to two full, championship-caliber teams. The program boasts strong performance records including multiple Divisional Championships, over a dozen Tournament Championships, and multiple appearances at Tournament finals.

"Above all, we are a family — one that plays together, grows together, and supports one another unconditionally."
